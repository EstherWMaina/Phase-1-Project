Welcome to Soko Yetu

Soko Yetu offers farmers a platform to sell their products and consumers a platform to buy fresh and quality products.

Farmers

White meat products

From Thika, our farmers have a wide range of white meat products: eggs, chicken, rabbit, pork and fish.

Irrigation produce

From Tana River, our farmers offer fresh produce entailing; watermelon, garlic, onions and green grams.

Tuber Vegetables

Located in Kinangop,the farmer offers: potatoes, yams, cassavas and sweet potatoes.
